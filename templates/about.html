About

IsiXhosa.xyz is a free, online live dictionary from Xhosa to English. The database of words is licensed under under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License. To start searching for a word, type either the English or the Xhosa word in the search bar and live results will be shown. A word can be clicked on to find more information about the word, such as examples and related words. A word can be submitted by going to the Submit A Word tab and then entering all the information about a word. It will then be reviewed by moderators and eventually accepted or rejected.
