Privacy policy

Information we collect

We collect your email, username, and Google OpenID Connect id. This is so we can 1) contact you in case of any issues regarding your account or submissions 2) credit you for your edits to the dictionary. This is stored on our server in the database. The username is also mirrored to the public GitHub archive, in order to provide accreditation.

Your email is not disclosed to anyone except moderators and administrators of the site.

Your user id (generated by the website) and username (entered by you) is uploaded publicly to GitHub, in order to credit your submissions. If you choose to forgo crediting, then your name will not be displayed publicly on any page.

If you decide that you no longer wish to be credited, your name will be hidden and it will not be uploaded in future. However, it will remain in the history of the repository.

If you decide that you do wish to be credited, your name will be shown on future and past edits.
